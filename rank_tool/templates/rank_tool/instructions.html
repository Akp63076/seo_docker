    Instructions to upload file

    · Format of the file to be uploaded should strictly be .csv (comma seperated values UTF-8 format).

    · The first column of the file should have header "Keywords" followed by the keywords you want to get the ranking for.

    · Keywords in any other column other than first column won't be ranked.

    · Once the keywords are ranked you will recieve the files in your mentioned email-id . You need not do anything after clicking send email button.

    · Once you click on the send email button your file would be line up in a queue which is available on the Queue Page.

    · Once you click on the send email there's no going back. Upload with caution.

    · Time uploaded doesn't mean time started, your file will start when previous file is processed.

    · Please be patient after uploading your file, the tool processes one file at time.

    General Information

    · You will get ranking for the following sites- collegedunia.com , prepp.in , skiksha.com , carrers360.com

    · The status column on queue page has 4 states-

    1. In Queue - Your file has succesfully been uploaded and is waiting for other files in queue to complete.

    2. In Progess - Your file is being processed.

    3. Complete - Your file has been ranked and you will recive the email shortly.

    4. Email Sent - Email has been sent to your given email id
